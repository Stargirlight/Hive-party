the hive party essentials

The Hive Party is an annual vibrant music experience by Ogarecords Hive Culture, showcasing live performances, DJ sets, and cultural showcases in one unforgettable night.

When
The hive party will take place on 9th November, 2025. Save the date and join the buzz!
Where
The hive party will hold at Empresa Hotel before LBS, Lekki, Lagos.

Expect electrifying performances from emerging talents and special headline acts such as Fola, Duncan Mighty, Famous Pluto, and lot's more that bring the Hive alive!

The Hive Party starts at 6pm. We recommend arriving early to secure the best spots and enjoy the full experience from start to finish.
